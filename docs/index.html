Dragon Slayer Fitness

Description:

In this project users can create their own fitness program with exercises they have choosen and monitor their proper implementation. Users can also record in their own food diary what and when they have eaten, thus keeping a close watch on their calories. The site also has a built-in calculator to count the calories needed per day to achieve the desired effect. Last but not least, site users can keep an eye on the muscular growth of each part of their body determining by themselves time periods of these checks. The administrative part is divided into three parts: a Food Administrator who has the authority to perform CRUD operations on food; an Exercise Administrator who has the authority to perform CRUD operations on exercises and a Head Administrator who can ban users and grant administrative rights to users.

Languages used to build the site: C# HTML CSS
